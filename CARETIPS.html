SPORTS EQUIPMENT CARE

Protect Your Gear, Improve Your Performance

1. Why Equipment Care Matters

Taking proper care of sports equipment helps improve performance, increase durability and ensure player safety. Regular maintenance can prevent unnecessary damage.

2. Cricket Equipment Care

Keep cricket bats in a dry place. Clean gloves, pads and helmets after every use and store them properly.

3. Football Equipment Care

Clean footballs and shoes after every practice session. Wash jerseys regularly and keep them in a dry place.

4. Boxing Equipment Care

Wipe boxing gloves after each use and allow them to air dry before storing them.

5. Chess Equipment Care

Store chess boards and pieces in a protective box and keep them away from water and dust.

6. Proper Storage Tips

Store all sports equipment in a cool, clean and dry place. Avoid stacking heavy items on top of your gear.

7. Safety Inspection

Check your equipment before every use and replace damaged items immediately.

8. Our Commitment

SPORTS FREAK encourages every player to maintain their equipment properly for better performance and safety.

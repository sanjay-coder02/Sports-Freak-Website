REFUND POLICY

1. Refund Eligibility

At SPORTS FREAK, customer satisfaction is important to us. Customers may request a refund within 7 days of receiving their order if the product is damaged, defective or incorrect.

2. Return Conditions

Products must be returned in their original condition, unused and with all tags, packaging and accessories included. Items that show signs of use may not qualify for a refund.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Customized products, clearance items and products damaged due to improper use by the customer are not eligible for refund or replacement.

4. Refund Process

Once the returned product is received and inspected, SPORTS FREAK will notify the customer about the approval or rejection of the refund request.

5. Refund Timeline

Approved refunds will be processed through the original payment method within 5 to 10 business days, depending on the payment provider.

6. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are generally non-refundable unless the return is caused by an error on our side, such as sending an incorrect or damaged product.

7. Cancellation Policy

Orders may be cancelled before they are shipped. Once an order has been dispatched, cancellation requests may not be accepted.

8. Contact for Refund Support

For any refund, return or cancellation queries, customers can contact SPORTS FREAK customer support through the contact details provided on the website.

Thank You.!
